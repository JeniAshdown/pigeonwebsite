How to get pigeons to trust you

Peanuts. Buy thousands of peanuts. Millions, possibly.

Find a pigeon. Go near it. Put a little food down.

More pigeons will come. Put food in your hand and place it near to the ground.

Slowly raise your hand to your lap. The pigeons will now trust you and jump up.

Congratulations. You are now the trusted pigeon feeder.
