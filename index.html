Pigeons, also known as Columba livia domestica or Rock Doves, are some of the most ridiculous birds that grace our British lands. Although commonly thought of as disease spreading, AKA "Flying Rats", this has since been proven to be false and Pigeons pose no greater threat than any other common bird.

This website has been designed as a guide to help you understand the following:

Why Pigeons are ridiculous

Why you should want them to trust you

How to do it
